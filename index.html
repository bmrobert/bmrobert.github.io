Compass

Internal Compass API that provides store and brand performance data.

Brands

Identifying information for brands in the Constellation Brands portfolio. Includes brands, sub-brands, and owners.

Items

Item data including classification, GTIN, UPC, packaging, and origin.

Returns promotional product images ("bottle shots") based on GTIN or UPC.

Worker Demographics

Basic identification data for Constellation employees and 3rd party partners. Includes contact information for easy account setup in external systems.
